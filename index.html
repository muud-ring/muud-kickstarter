Common questions.

What people ask first.

When does the Kickstarter go live?

June 15. Everyone on this list gets one direct email the moment the campaign opens. No social-feed lottery, no countdown anxiety.

Is this a wellness gadget or a medical device?

The MUUD Ring is a consumer wellness device. It is not a diagnostic instrument and is not a substitute for clinical care. For patients who need it, MUUD Clinic provides telepsychiatry on the same rail — but the ring itself is wellness, by design and by regulation.

Do I need the app to use the ring?

Yes. The ring is the sensor layer; the app is the reflection layer. The loop only closes when both are present. The app is free on iOS and Android — your backing covers the hardware.

How does MUUD handle my data?

Biometric data is encrypted in transit and at rest. The MUUD Clinic side is HIPAA-aligned. We do not sell data, and you can export or delete everything at any time. Full privacy notice published before the Kickstarter goes live.
